## Analysis
- Floating rich-text toolbar with a soft shadow and pill shape, hovering above the paragraph it edits.
- "Ask AI" gets a gradient pink chip at the far left — the only color in an otherwise monochrome bar, so the AI action reads as primary without a sparkle cliché.
- Grouped controls with hairline dividers: font/size, color dot, B/I/U, alignment. Clear rhythm.
- Live tooltip ("Bold: ⌘ + B") teaches the shortcut on hover — small, dark, unobtrusive.
- Body text below fades to lighter grey toward the end — focus stays on the edited line.

## Steal this
Give the AI action one deliberate color chip in an otherwise grayscale toolbar — it signals "primary" more honestly than a sparkle icon.
