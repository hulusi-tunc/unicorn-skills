## Their words
"before after"

## Analysis
- A redesign study: cramped "before" nutrition home vs. a breathing "after" — the improvement IS the point.
- After wins by hierarchy: one hero radial calorie gauge with a big "1232 calories remaining" numeral replaces the before's small tucked-away dial.
- Data grouped into labelled cards (Macros Breakdown, Active Calories) with clear section titles, instead of the before's stacked undifferentiated blocks.
- Restrained color: greyscale surfaces, macro data carries the only color (green/blue/orange arcs), one red active-calories bar.
- More whitespace, fewer borders, a floating bottom tab bar with an active-state pill.

## Steal this
When redesigning a dense screen, promote one metric to a hero visual (the radial gauge + huge numeral) and demote the rest into labelled cards — hierarchy is the redesign.
