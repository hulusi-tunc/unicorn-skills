## Analysis
- Two versions of one profile card side by side — a refinement study, not two ideas.
- Right version reads cleaner: stats moved into a soft contained pill, avatar gains a rainbow "experience" ring (the left's separate "exp." bar folded into the avatar), follow becomes a compact "+".
- Light, airy: soft cloud cover photo, generous rounding, near-black numerals over grey labels.
- Social icons stripped to monochrome glyphs, evenly spaced — no dividers on the right version.
- Consolidation is the move: same data, fewer containers, one decorative element doing double duty.

## Steal this
When refining a card, fold separate elements into one (the exp. bar became the avatar ring) instead of adding — subtraction reads as "designed."
