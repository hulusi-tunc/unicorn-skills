## Analysis
- Layout: full-bleed photography drives every screen — login is a single edge-to-edge action shot, onboarding stacks a hero image over a dark list, home leads with a photo-backed banner. Image is the layout, not a decoration inside it.
- Type: condensed uppercase display face (compressed grotesque) for headlines — "LOGIN", "CHOOSE YOUR TEAMS", "JOIN THE ATLANTA SPORTS" — against a plain sans for body/labels. Strong two-face contrast.
- Color: near-black surfaces, high-energy lime-green primary CTA, warm secondary accents (coral/amber stat tiles) pulled from the sports photography.
- Structure: dark list rows with circular team logos and trailing reward chips; two-up stat tiles with corner action arrows; bottom tab bar with one highlighted AI action.
- Mood: energetic, premium, sporty — confident scale, motion-blur imagery, real depth.

## Steal this
Let a single full-bleed photo carry the screen and set the palette, then sit condensed uppercase display type directly on it — image as layout, not image in a card.
